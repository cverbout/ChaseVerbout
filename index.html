Who is Chase V?

Hey there! I'm Chase Verbout, a graduate student in Computer Science at Portland State University, and a bit of a tech enthusiast. My world revolves around programming and problem-solving, with a particular knack for Python and C++. My days are mostly filled with tutoring and supporting undergrads at PSU, a role that keeps me connected to the basics and constantly learning.

Professionally, my experiences range from rigorous academic coursework to hands-on internships, including an exciting stint at NASA. There, I delved into in-space robotics and simulations, a venture that perfectly aligned with my passion for space and technology.

Outside the binary world of programming, I'm an avid explorer of the great outdoors, often hiking Oregon's majestic mountains. I'm also a basketball fan and a Dungeon Master in D&D sessions, hobbies that provide a perfect counterbalance to my tech-centric life.

Looking forward, I'm intrigued by the possibilities in game development and space exploration. These fields blend my technical skills with my curiosity for what lies beyond the known, offering thrilling prospects for my future.

That's a bit about me, Chase V. Just a student, a tech enthusiast, and an adventurer at heart. Thanks for dropping by!
