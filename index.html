CheckTheFridge

CommonPaths

Webpage

CommonPaths is an impressive multi-tenant GIS recording and reconciliation system that revolutionizes the collection, processing, and distribution of pedestrian pathway data. This innovative solution provides users with access to high-quality information about pedestrian pathways in a standardized and computable format. Notably, CommonPaths excels in accurately describing, translating, and serving the data, making it accessible and actionable for a wide range of applications and users, with a particular focus on enhancing accessibility for individuals with mobility impairments. Throughout my involvement in this project, I gained invaluable experience in project planning, creating detailed technical documentation, and assembling the project's webpage, all of which have further refined my skills and expertise in these areas.
